Game Rules

How to Play

Simple rules to enter the world of bluffing

1

Setup

Shuffle the 17 cards (excluding Young Kukunochi / No.0) face-down to form the Draw Pile.
Place Young Kukunochi sideways next to the pile as the "Rebirth Card".
Each player draws 1 card as their hand. Decide who goes first, then proceed clockwise.

Shuffle the cards well to keep the game fair
2

Turn Flow

1. Draw 1 card from the draw pile, giving you 2 cards.
2. Choose 1 card to play face-up and activate its ability.
3. Place the used card in your discard area (left to right).
4. After resolving the ability, your turn ends. Next player clockwise.

Cards discarded by opponents' abilities do not activate
3

Elimination

Eliminated players must reveal their hand and add it to their discard area.

4

Victory Conditions

The game ends in one of these ways:

1. Draw pile runs out → Remaining players reveal hands; highest number wins. Tied = draw.

2. Last player standing → Instant victory for the survivor.

3. All eliminated simultaneously → Draw.
